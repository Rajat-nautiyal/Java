Flatten a Sorted Linked List — merge, one column at a time

Every column is already sorted top-to-bottom via bottom pointers; columns are chained left-to-right via next. The trick: repeatedly merge the flattened-so-far list with the next column, exactly like merging two sorted lists — just walking bottom instead of next.
